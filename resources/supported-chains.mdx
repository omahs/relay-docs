Relay is currently supported on the following chains. Please reach out to learn more about how to add Relay to your chain.

## Supported Chains

### Mainnets

- Ethereum
- Zora
- Base
- Arbitrum
- Arbitrum Nova
- Optimism

### Testnets

- Sepolia
- Goerli
- Base Goerli
- Zora Goerli
- Frame testnet

## Adding Relay to Your Chain

Relay can easily be added to any EVM-chain, such as OP stack rollups, Arbitrum Orbit chains, or more. Please feel free to [Reach Out](mailto:support@reservoir.tools) to discuss getting you chain supported today!
